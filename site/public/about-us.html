About us

A civil contracting business built on dependable delivery.

LED Plant & Civil is a privately established civil contracting business delivering reliable civil works, earthworks, underground services and plant hire solutions across Southeast Queensland.

Built on strong foundations and practical industry experience, the company has grown through a commitment to doing the job right, building trust with clients, and maintaining high standards of safety, quality and accountability.

Company overview

Safe, compliant and technically precise outcomes.

From early works through to project handover, LED Plant & Civil combines experienced personnel, modern and well maintained plant, and disciplined construction methodologies to deliver safe, compliant and technically precise outcomes.

Supported by a capable operations and leadership team, we take pride in our transparent approach, consistent performance and focus on long-term partnerships, ensuring every project is delivered with professionalism, reliability and care.
