## Page 5 — About / Careers

### About us
LED Plant & Civil is a privately established civil contracting business delivering reliable civil works, earthworks, underground services, and plant hire solutions across Southeast Queensland. Built on strong foundations and practical industry experience, the company has grown through a commitment to doing the job right, building trust with clients, and maintaining high standards of safety, quality, and accountability.

From early works through to project handover, LED Plant & Civil combines experienced personnel, modern and well maintained plant, and disciplined construction methodologies to deliver safe, compliant, and technically precise outcomes. Supported by a capable operations and leadership team, we take pride in our transparent approach, consistent performance, and focus on long-term partnerships - ensuring every project is delivered with professionalism, reliability, and care.

### Mission
Our mission is to deliver dependable, high-quality civil construction and plant hire solutions that our clients can trust. From early works through to project handover, LED Plant & Civil is committed to combining skilled people, modern equipment, and disciplined construction methods to deliver safe, compliant, and technically sound outcomes - done right the first time.

We strive to build long-term partnerships through transparency, accountability, and consistent performance, while upholding a safety-first culture and a commitment to continuous improvement. By investing in our people, systems, and equipment, we aim to support our clients’ success and contribute positively to the communities and infrastructure we help build.

### Vision
Our vision is to be a trusted and respected civil contracting partner, recognised for delivering safe, high-quality infrastructure works with consistency, integrity, and technical excellence. We aim to set the standard for dependable delivery in the civil construction sector by continuously improving how we plan, operate, and support our clients’ projects.

Through investment in our people, equipment, and management systems, we seek to grow sustainably while maintaining the values that define who we are - safety without compromise, transparency in everything we do, and a commitment to building infrastructure that performs for the long term.

### Core values

#### Quality Workmanship & Technical Excellence
LED delivers quality workmanship through skilled professionals, state of the art equipment, precise civil construction methods, and a commitment to technical excellence that ensures consistent and reliable outcomes, done right the first time.

#### Accountability and Trust - Through Complete Transparency

#### Safety Without Compromise

#### Continuous Improvement & Growth
